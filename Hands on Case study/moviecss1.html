Vanilla Sky

Vanilla sky is a psychological triller starring Tom Cruise, Cameron Diaz, and Penelope Cruz. The main protagonist Tom Cruise is a self-indulget and vain publishing magnate finds his privileged life upended after a vehicular accident with a resentful lover.The film's storyline is a blend of science-fiction and reality, and is a mystery for viewers to solve through Tom Cruises's character David Ames's perspective.
